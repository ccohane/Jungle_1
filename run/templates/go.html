Jungle

Find Your Beast

Jungle recommends restaurants to you based off of your food preferences and finds restaurants that similar users also like or dislike. Leave some reviews and jungle will be able to find you better restaurants that fit your needs.

Jungle uses a machine learning algorithm called matrix factorization to determine the best restaurants for you. Soon, Jungle will be able to analyze your written reviews to determine your star rating based off of your reviews wording.
